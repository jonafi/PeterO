About Peter

About Peter

Born August 12, 1953 in Minnesota, Peter grew up in the Ukrainian community of northeast Minneapolis. He was a regular on violin, mandolin and guitar for many years in the house band of Garrison Keillor's "A Prairie Home Companion" radio show.
